Product Transfer Process

Transferring product between stores is a necessary process that requires care and attention to detail. Follow this guide and take your time while performing transfers

  1. Create PO on Outgoing Side

    1. Set supplier to Store you are transferring to
    2. Enter Product you wish to transfer
    3. Enter quantity and cost.
      1. Cost from Cost Average
      2. Outgoing quantity negative
    4. If notes are required, use product code "..." and modify description
    5. Tax Code "No Tax"
    6. Record PO#

    Example of an Outgoing PO.

    Location of Cost Average on a product card.

  2. Create PO on Incoming Side

    1. Set supplier to store you are transferring from
    2. Enter products
    3. Enter quantity and cost
      1. Cost from Outgoing PO
      2. Incoming positive quantity
    4. Tax Code "No Tax"
    5. Enter Outgoing PO# in Ref field
    6. Enter Incoming PO in Ref of the associated PO

  3. Verify Total is the same on both documents

  4. Receive Out

    1. Negatively receive outgoing products, with associated SN as needed
    2. Attach a physical copy of the PO to the outgoing product

  5. Receive In upon arrival

Things to Remember

POs must have the EXACT SAME quantities and costs

DO NOT receive out products at $0.00 unless that is the REAL average cost of goods as reflected in Lightspeed

DO NOT move product between location without following these steps.
