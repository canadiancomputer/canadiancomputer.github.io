Trade-In Process

Customer Brings in computer for Trade-In

  1. Verify Pricing - GBB or Trade
    • If GBB, 50% of original sell price, or 50% off the new unit they wish to purchase, whichever is lower.
    • If not GBB, retrieve pricing from Trade-In Portal
  2. Create SRO
    • Input Serial Number to retrieve device details
    • In the problem description write:
      • Quoted buyback/trade-in amount
      • Full specs of machine: Processor/Memory/Hard Drive/Graphics
      • Change to Status 23 Trade Test (Carbon Computing only)
      • Any deficiencies (missing adapter/keyboard/mouse) should be noted
      • Deliver to the customer an expected 24-Hour turnaround on Trade Testing in normal circumstance
  3. Have customer sign out of any Find My Device

Retail Contact

  • If the unit passes:
    1. Contact customer, inform them of the Trade PASS at full value.
      1. If the customer wishes to migrate to a new machine, update the notes in the SRO, reserve the new computer on an ORDER tied to the SRO. Inform technicians of migration.
      2. If the customer wishes to be paid out via EFT/Cheque, complete step 2, then hand off to Management.
    2. Create a Trade-In Acceptance Form (Core 1)(Carbon), have customer return a signed copy.
    3. Hand off to Management for SKU creation
  • If the unit has deficiencies
    1. Contact customer, inform them of the deficiencies and price adjustments (adjustments provided by management).
      1. If the customer wishes to proceed with applied adjustments, continue as per Trade PASS
      2. If the customer does not wish to proceed, update the notes in the SRO and mark Ready for Pickup.

Technician Migrate/Trade Prep

  1. If the customer is transferring to a new machine
    1. Pull the reserved computer from inventory, migrate data
    2. Contact customer and inform them of the completed transfer
    3. Wait 7-10 days, then follow Step 2.
  2. If the customer is not purchasing a new machine/after 1(c)
    1. Perform clean install of latest/most reasonable OS for given machine
    2. Perform physical cleaning of the unit
    3. Update management that unit is ready for sale
