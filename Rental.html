Rental Process

Setting up a rental requires two documents. A Rental Allocation Order and a Rental Fee Order. The Rental Allocation Order is used to reserve the product being rented, and communicate the value of the items being rented to the customer. The Rental Fee Order is invoiced to charge the customer for the rental. Below are instructions for setting up both documents.
